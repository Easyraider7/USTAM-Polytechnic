Empowering Future Leaders with Knowledge and Skills

Welcome to USTAM Polytechnic Osogbo, a leading institution of higher learning dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement. Located in Osun State, Nigeria, our polytechnic has a proud history of providing quality education and empowering students to achieve their full potential. We offer a diverse range of programs across various disciplines, including Engineering, Business, Industrial and Applied Science, Environment and Design Technology, and Applied Arts. Our curriculum is designed to provide a robust theoretical foundation while emphasizing hands-on training and practical experience.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Its your rights to have your questions answered

Here, we have compiled answers to the
most commonly asked questions to help you navigate your journey with us. Whether you're a prospective student looking for information about admissions, a current student seeking details on tuition fees, or an alumni wanting to stay connected, you'll find the answers you need here. If you have additional questions, please don't hesitate to Contact Us.

Prospective students can apply through our online application portal. The application process includes submitting necessary documents, meeting the program-specific requirements, and paying the application fee. Visit our Admissions Page for more details

The polytechnic provides on-campus accommodation facilities. Details about the types of accommodation, application process, and fees will be discussed when student pays the tuition fee.

Tuition fees vary depending on the program and level of study. A detailed breakdown of the fees for each program can be made available at the schools bursary office
